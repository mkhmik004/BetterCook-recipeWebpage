Better Oatmeal PanCakes

Ingridients

  1. Oats
  2. Milk of choice
  3. Eggs
  4. Butter/oil
  5. Sugar of choice
  6. All Purpose Flour
  7. Baking Powder
  8. salt
  9. Ground Cinnamon(optional)
  10. Vannila Essence

Instructions

  1. in bowl, combine the oats and milk. Allow this mixture to sit for a few minutes to soften the oats.
  2. In the same bowl, add flour, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon (if using) then mix.
  3. In another bowl (2nd bowl), whisk together the eggs, melted butter or oil, sugar, and vanilla essence.
  4. mix the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix(it's okay if there are a few lumps.)
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  6. Pour a ladleful of the pancake batter onto the hot skillet for each pancake. Cook until you see bubbles forming on the surface and the edges start to set,
  7. Flip the pancakes and cook the other side until golden brown
  8. Serve your delicious oatmeal pancakes hot with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup/honey, fresh fruit, yogurt, or whipped cream.

Notes

To store the PanCakes, first let them cool completely, then separate them with parchment paper. Wrap the stack tightly with plastic wrap or aluminum foil, or store them in an airtight container. Label and date the container, then freeze for up to 2-3 months or refridgerate for upto 5 days. Reheat frozen pancakes in a toaster, microwave, or oven, and add your favorite toppings for a delicious breakfast anytime.

These oatmeal pancakes are not only easy to make but also nutritious, filling and perfect for meal preperation in advance, making them a perfect choice for a hearty breakfast or brunch anytime. Enjoy!
